About Rubina

I am Rubina Mansuri…and helping people live a fulfilled ,content, healthy life is my calling.

My journey with enabling people achieve their best potential started early - even as a young and aspiring fashion designer, I always wanted to help people feel good by looking good.

I believe that the body grows, but the soul is a miraculous light, ever playful and young. Only when people learn to accept themselves as they are will their true beauty shine through.

My Journey with RTT

As a child, and throughout my teen years I was plagued with health problems ranging from the niggling to the major. I suffered from poor self-image and poor self-confidence. One of my earliest and most traumatic memories is of shivering with fear when waiting for my turn to read out a passage from the English text book in school.

These minor health problems started escalating and finally the time when I developed Rheumatism was the accelerated decline of my health - mentally and physically. The wheelchair became my companion, and my eye sight was nearly gone. No amount of treatment or diet restrictions helped. Though I had learnt to live with RA, accepting it as inevitable and resigning myself to my fate, losing my eyesight was literally the worst moment of my life…& I felt like a victim!..helpless & worthless

But from the depths of my despair, feeling completely hopeless crumbling with no sense of internal power came the turning point of my life…..to understand relationship between the experiences of our past and chronic illnesses.

From there on there was no looking back as I started studying more and more about the human body, food, supplements, exercises, yoga, soul-mind-body connection & eventually got to know about pioneer Marisa Peer and that was the beginning of a transformation beyond imagination. A transformation that catapulted me towards achieving my ultimate purpose in life - helping people and guiding them.

I worked steadily towards becoming a qualified practitioner of RTT and after extensively studying the principles and techniques along with constant practice, today here I am…
