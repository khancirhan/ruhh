What is Rapid Transformation Therapy?

Rapid Transformational Therapy (RTT) is as its name suggests - a therapy that transforms the person very quickly.

The basic premise of RTT is that “if we can do this to ourselves, we can undo it as well” - basically implying that the root cause of all illness lies within the labyrinth of our own thoughts and experiences - past and present.

RTT actually is as several doctors have called it “practical neuroscience”. It involves reprogramming the neural pathways that have always told us “I am not good enough, or I can’t do this” and more such deep rooted, negative self-talk to a better way of being, a more healthy, positive way of being - achieving our true potential, our self-esteem and belief in ourselves.

Study of the human brain and mind is an ever-evolving field, and research into the connection between chronic health issues and emotional trauma is now quite well-documented. This research categorically states that our early, unhealed mental wounds and trauma leave us susceptible and vulnerable to pain. It’s like hauling the excess and unnecessary weight of our entire personal history in our cells and nervous system - a surefire way to self-sabotage our health and life in general.

RTT thus is a range of techniques that not only help you sort the current problem - whatever it is…but also enable you to achieve a life of fulfilment and good health on a long-term basis. It is a guide for people to solve a variety of problems that could touch any and every aspect of their life - be it emotional, physical, financial, social or even spiritual.

Also, though the broad spectrum of problems may be the same on the surface - chronic health issues, financial difficulties, relationship struggles and so on, but - and this is a very big but - each client’s problem requires to be dealt with on a very individual basis. There is certainly no “one size fits all” method that can be used.

What also distinguishes RTT from psychological counselling or any other such therapy or treatment is the effects achieved within as little as 90 minutes.
